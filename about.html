Who We Are

Our Inspiration

The Zephaniah Owidi Odong(ZOO) Memorial Foundation is inspired by the life of The Late Zephaniah Owidi Odongo who:

  • Grew up in extreme poverty in rural Kenya.
  • Through education, dedication and the good will of others in supporting his determination, overcame serious adversity, excelled in his scholarship.
  • Became a distinguished Electrical Engineer who contributed significantly to the Kenyan Telecommunications industry and performed activities supported by this organization.
  • Believed and demonstrated that education really is the key out of poverty, and that technology bridges the world.

Our Objectives

The Zoo Memorial Foundation was established in September 2008 with the following objectives:

  • Work to close the digital divide in Kenya
  • Collect, refurbish and donate computers to under-served areas in Kenya
  • Enable access to information for learning, communication and IT skill-set development

What we do

  1. Set up ICT centers – Beneficiaries of The ZOO Memorial Foundation have fully functional ICT centers with donated computers and internet connection.
  2. Training – The foundation provides and facilitates computer literacy training to its beneficiary center trainers and members of the community.
  3. Creating awareness – Societies around beneficiary centers are encouraged and enabled to take part in ICT activities such as using message boards, chat rooms and email.
  4. Advocacy – Sensitizing local companies and organizations on the importance and participation in digital revolution in Kenya.

Why ICT centers?

A number of converging trends make our foundation's Rural ICT Project relevant in ICT facilitation and accessibility especially in rural Kenya.

  • Increased use of ICT for both economic and social development.
  • Growth of mobile telephony and mobile service providers.
  • Increased necessity for use of ICT and limited skilled persons.
  • Limited or no ICT access to majority of rural Kenya.
  • Technology shy – we see this as an opportunity to make changes within an evolving industry.

How we do it

  • We partner with schools and community based organizations
  • Set up ICT centers by donating computers
  • We train the trainers
