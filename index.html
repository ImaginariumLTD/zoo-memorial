Mission:

To enable access to ICT and scholarship through the donation of computers, provision of internet services and facilitation of computer literacy training to schools, community centers and youth groups in Kenya.

Overview

The Zephaniah Owidi Odongo(ZOO) Memorial Foundation is a non-profit organization providing access to information communication technology in rural and underserved areas in Kenya.

The foundation is a progressive organization whose goal is to improve social welfare and create sustainable economic development

Objectives

The Zoo Memorial Foundation was established in September 2008 with the following objectives:

  • Work to close the digital divide in Kenya
  • Collect, refurbish and donate computers to underserved areas in Kenya, rural schools, youth organizations and community facilities
  • To enable access to information for learning communication and IT skill-set development

Our Values:

  • Equity
  • Partnership
  • Sponshorship
  • Integrity
  • Voluntary Service
