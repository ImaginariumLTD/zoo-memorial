Projects

What we aim to achieve

The Zoo Memorial Foundation's projects and initiatives are community based with clear objectives

  • Gain a usage rate of 100 users daily
  • Impart ICT skills training to 1000 persons annually

What the community gains

  • Access to ICT — The ICT centers will provide accessible areas for the community to utilise information communications technology.
  • Exposure — ICT will provide opportunities for exposure and encourage repeat visits to the centers enabling worldwise interactions.
  • Training — The ICT centers will offr affordable training in efforts to increase computer literacy in undeserved and rural areas.
  • Network — The foundation will build a strong community of people who have similar interests. This will allow for the exchange of information for development.

Rural ICT Project

Background

With the intention of being a progressive organization that improves the social welfare and creates sustainable economic development, the ZOO Memorial Foundation enables access to ICT and scholarship by providing internet services and facilitation of computer literacy training to schools, community centres and youth groups in Kenya.

Objectives

  • Identifying and approaching possible partner organizations
  • Establishing ICT centers
  • Providing support to beneficiary centers
  • Assisting the centers to develop and implement business development plans in order to ensure that they are financially autonomous

Beneficiaries

  • ACK Emmanuel Karuri
  • Eng. ZOO Memorial Foundation Community Computer Center
  • Kibera Community Development Agenda(KCODA)
  • St. Boniface Magare Secondary School

Mobile ICT

Background

Aligned with our intentions of being a progressive organization, the foundation has an established mobile ICT unit to reach underserved areas in the country. The unit offers internet, printing and photocopying services to the community members in exchange for a small fee.

Objectives

  • Bring the services of a regular ICT center to the people using a mobile unit
  • Increase accessibility and reach of ICT centers in underserved areas
  • Revenue generating activity to support the activities of the Foundation

Elite Sponsorships and Adverts

The foundation will provide custom sponsorship and advertisement packages to interested sponsors in exchange for paying fixed monthly fees sponsors will receive premium placement of banners on the mobile unit for advertising.
