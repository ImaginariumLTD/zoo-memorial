The Team

We are driven by the intense energy harnessed from a diverse pool of professionals who passionately care about our mission

June Odongo – Founder

June Odongi is a Kenyan born software engineer residing in Boston, Massachusettes in the United States. She holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Massachusetts at Lowell and is currently pursuring her MBA at Harvard University.

She joined EMC in 2005 where she worked in several engineering capactities and also held the position of Product manager. June founded the ZOO Memorial Foundation in 2008 in memory of her late father, Eng. Zephaniah Owidi Odongo.

June is a member of the United Nations Global Alliance for ICT and Development(UNGAID).

Nelly Odongo – Managing Trustee

Nelly is the Managing Trustee of The ZOO Memorial Foundation where she is responsible for planning and monitoring the foundation's projects. She holds a Bachelor's degree in Commerce from the Catholic University of Eastern Africa with a specialization in Human Resource Management.

Nelly has collective work in adminstration and management from business process outsourcing and marketing research firms

Clement Mwangi – Advising Trustee

Clement is a Trustee of The ZOO Memorial Foundation, he holds a Bachelor's degree in Finance from the University of Nantes and a Master's degree in Educational Planning from the University of Rene Descartes in Paris, France.

Clement has worked as a consultant in managing education at the International Institute of Educational Planning(IIEP) of UNESCO and the Inter-agency network for Education in Emergencies(INEE) in Paris. In October 2005, he was elected President of UNESCO Youth Forum and returned to Kenya where he helped set up UNESCO Youth Forum -Kenya Chapter with the Ministry of Education.

Clement is an independent consultant in educational planning and youth development and the Founder and Director of Psilon Limited, a leading translation and interpretation firm set up in 2006.
